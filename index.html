Iridescence cultural week Online!
Iridescence Cultural week

Iridescence cultural week Online! carmel Convent 4 2nd place and 1 time 3rd Iridescence Carmel Convent School’s Annual Cultural fest is conducted each year with great enthusiasm and success. And this year because of the pandemic Carmel conducted it online from 17th to 25th August 2020. It was a completely new but exciting experience for everyone. The regular favourite events like “Book Discussion”, “Meraki”, “Shutterbug” were all conducted online and many new events like “Restyle The Robe”, “Ad-Beat”,”Mask Making” and “Ingenium” were also introduced. Almost 25 schools participated in the five day program. It gave students a chance to showcase their creativity and talent on a completely new platform. The event was a great success and a learning experience for all!
